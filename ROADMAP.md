ORBX Roadmap

> ORBX is a Stellar-based utility token issued by Orion Group. This roadmap outlines planned development phases focused on functional utility, transparency, and long-term sustainability. All timelines are indicative and subject to change.




---

Phase 1 — Asset Foundation

Objective: Establish ORBX as a verifiable Stellar asset with clear metadata and transparent control separation.

ORBX issuance on the Stellar network

Separation of issuer and distributor accounts

Fixed maximum supply defined at issuance

Publication of official stellar.toml

Hosting of token logo and branding assets

Submission and verification on Stellar Expert



---

Phase 2 — Public Launch & Distribution

Objective: Enable transparent access and on-chain discoverability.

Initial token distribution via Stellar DEX

Market-driven price discovery

Public disclosure of allocation structure

On-chain visibility of all token movements

Launch of official community channels



---

Phase 3 — ORBX Betting Utility

Objective: Activate core functional utility through a non-custodial betting platform.

Beta launch of Orion Group betting platform

ORBX used as the interaction token for betting activities

Wallet-based access using Stellar-compatible wallets

On-chain settlement using predefined execution logic

No centralized custody of user funds



---

Phase 4 — Orion Group Membership Utility

Objective: Provide service-based access within the Orion Group ecosystem.

ORBX-enabled access to Orion Group digital platforms

Entry to private community channels

Eligibility for Orion Group tools and services

No ownership, equity, or profit rights conveyed



---

Phase 5 — Governance & Community Participation

Objective: Encourage community involvement without financial obligations.

Non-financial polls and feedback mechanisms

Community feature and utility proposals

Advisory and non-binding participation model

Periodic transparency and progress updates



---

Phase 6 — Long-Term Sustainability

Objective: Maintain relevance and alignment beyond market cycles.

Continuous utility development

Expansion of ORBX use cases within Orion Group

Alignment with Stellar ecosystem standards

Responsible, compliance-aware growth strategy



---

Disclosures

ORBX is a utility token and does not represent equity, ownership, or guaranteed returns

Token value, if any, is determined by market activity

Users retain full custody of their wallets

Participation is voluntary and at user discretion
