Our Mission

Redefining cross-border payments and digital asset ownership on the world's most efficient blockchain.

Why We Built This

The Stellar network is built for speed and accessibility. Our token leverages these core strengths to provide a seamless bridge between traditional finance and the decentralized future. By utilizing Stellar Assets, we ensure every transaction is finalized in seconds.

Our goal is to eliminate the high fees and slow processing times of legacy systems. Whether you are an institution or a retail holder, Alnitak provides the infrastructure for a borderless economy.

Network Foundation

Powered by the Stellar Consensus Protocol (SCP) for energy-efficient validation.

Transparency

Alnitak token is Permanently capped at 100,000,000 tokens, 25% of which is used to add liquidity. All issuance and distribution details are verified on-chain via Stellar.expert.
